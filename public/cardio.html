Cardio

Exercise

Cardiovascular exercises, often referred to as cardio exercises or aerobic exercises, are a crucial component of a well-rounded fitness routine. These exercises focus on increasing your heart rate, breathing rate, and circulation, which collectively improve your cardiovascular health and boost your overall endurance. Cardio exercises encompass a wide range of activities that engage large muscle groups and require sustained rhythmic movements.
