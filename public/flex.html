Flex

Muscle

Flex muscle exercises, also known as isometric exercises, are a type of strength training that involves contracting and holding a specific muscle or group of muscles in a static position without any visible movement in the joints. Unlike dynamic exercises that involve joint movement, such as lifting weights or performing push-ups, flex muscle exercises focus on building strength, endurance, and muscle definition by exerting force against an immovable object or resisting force applied by an external source.
